Who are we?

The Feed my Lambs Youth Ministry (FLY-M) is an interdenominational Christian ministry under the covering of Christ Co-workers Fellowship (ChrisCo).
Its founders are Presbyter Kato and Pastor Joan Lafont. The Ministry bases its teaching, coaching, and training on Biblical principles. FLY-M reinforces this mandate mainly through outreaches to various learning institutions. For over 30 years (since 1987), FLY-M has been on the ground directly ministering to students across the country and this has been upon invitation by the respective institutions. Presbyter Kato used to get invites to primary schools to do pastoral programs during the week and that is how the work started. The very first schools in which FLY-M started outreach were the primary schools precisely Moi Avenue Primary School. Then there came Madaraka Primary school, Westlands Primary School among others within Nairobi. The more interaction he had with children in these institutions the more he had a burden for them and sometime later in 1989 he started getting invites to high schools to go preach in weekend challenges. So high school ministry begun. God begun to speak to Presbyter Kato strongly about ministry to young people during this time through the book of John 21 from verse 15 and this is where the name FLY-M was born from. Around 2005 FLY-M began Campus ministry. University of Nairobi and Kenyatta University were among the first and despite the challenges in outreach within the universities, came the birthing of Breach Repairers (BR)
